A.P.J. ABDUL KALAM(1931-2015)
FULL NAME: Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam
KNOWN AS: Missile Man Of India,People's President
DATE OF BIRTH: 15 October 1931
BIRTH PLACE: Rameshwaram,Tamilnadu,India
AWARDS: Bharat Ratan,Padam Vibhusan,Padam Bhusan,Hoover Medal,Ramanujan Medal,Veer Savarkar Award,King Charles II Medal etc.

The following list is a time line of Kalam Sir's Life :-

  • 1931:Born in Rameshwaram on Pamband Island,Tamilnadu(India)
  • 1960:Graduated from Madras Institute of Technology and joined Defence Research & Development Service(DRDO)
  • 1969:Transferred to Indian Space Research Organisation(ISRO)
  • 1980:As a project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III) which successfully deployed the Rohini satellite in near-earth orbit.
  • 1981:The Government of India honoured him with the "Padma Bhushan"
  • 1990:The Government of India honoured him with the "Padma Vibhushan"
  • 1992:Kalam served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and Secretary of the Defence Research and Development Organisation
  • 1997: Kalam received India's highest civilian honour, the Bharat Ratna, for his contribution to the scientific research and modernisation of defence technology in India.
  • 2002:He become President of India
  • 2012: Kalam Sir aunched a programme for the youth of India called the What Can I Give Movement, with a central theme of defeating corruption.
  • 2013: He was the recipient of the Von Braun Award from the National Space Society "to recognize excellence in the management and leadership of a space-related project".
  • 2015:Passed away when he travelled to Shillong to deliver a lecture on "Creating a Livable Planet Earth" at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong.
